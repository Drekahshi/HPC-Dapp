Executive Summary & Core Mission

HISA is a decentralized, AI-powered ecosystem for Africa's SDGs, focusing on mental health, conservation, data rights, and financial inclusion. Built on Hedera, it uses agentic AI, smart contracts, and liquidity pools for passive income, wellness resources, and tokenizing contributions. Supports online/offline access for 350M feature phone users, with privacy-preserving tech and community governance.
